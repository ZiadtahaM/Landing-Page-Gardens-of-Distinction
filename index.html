Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

During the growing season (spring through early fall), we recommend mowing your lawn once a week. In slower growth periods, every 10-14 days may be sufficient. Regular mowing promotes healthier grass and prevents weed growth.

What is the best time of year to plant new trees and shrubs?

Fall is generally the best time for planting most trees and shrubs as it allows roots to establish before winter. Early spring is also a good option. Avoid planting during the heat of summer when plants are under stress.

Do you offer organic lawn care options?

Yes, we offer organic lawn care programs that use natural fertilizers and pest control methods. These options are safer for children, pets, and the environment while still maintaining a beautiful, healthy lawn.

How long does a typical landscape design project take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. Simple garden installations might take 1-2 weeks, while complete landscape transformations can take 4-8 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you provide maintenance services after installation?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages to keep your landscape looking its best year-round. From regular lawn care to seasonal pruning and garden maintenance, we can customize a plan to fit your needs.
